About us

“We, the solar concern of Shree Mahaveer Traders aim to provide innovative power alternatives and future energy solutions. The firm's operation in service sector especially in the arena of renewable energy has remarkably produced happy and satisfied customers.. Promoting sustainable development and eco- friendly initiatives, we aim to provide efficient solar solutions to our valuable customers in commercial as well as non - commercial sectors. We are here to offer you with tailored solar solutions according to your necessity and requirements. Instead of readymade provisions, we believe in serving the unique needs and preferences of our customers in the best possible manner. We deal in multiple trusted brands - Adani, Waaree, Vikram, Goldi etc for panels and Havells, Polycab, Luminous , Ksolar, microtec etc for inverters .
